This site started as a static Gatsby portfolio. It served its purpose well: project write-ups, contact information, and a deployment pipeline that made a static site inexpensive to run.

The next version keeps that static-site spirit, but moves the foundation to Astro. The goal is to make the site simpler to maintain, easier to write for, and better suited to a personal blog without giving up the project archive.

## What is changing

- The site is moving from Gatsby to Astro.
- Project write-ups are becoming Astro content collection entries.
- The blog now supports MDX, tags, authors, reading time, table-of-contents navigation, and RSS.
- The visual direction is moving toward a cleaner portfolio layout with stronger blog ergonomics.

## What is staying

The site is still static-first, content-driven, and focused on projects, engineering notes, and ways to get in touch.
